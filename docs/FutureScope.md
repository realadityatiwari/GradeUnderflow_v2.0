# Future Scope

GradeUnderflow v1.0 establishes the foundation of an Academic Intelligence Platform. As we transition from MVP to a widespread consumer SaaS, the following features are planned for future iterations.

## AI Academic Advisor
Integrate an LLM-powered assistant directly into the dashboard. The assistant will have context of the student's historical `Evaluation` and `Prediction` data to offer actionable, personalized advice (e.g., "Focus on your upcoming CS201 Assignment, it's dragging down your SGPA target").

## Export & Reporting Engine
Provide PDF and CSV export capabilities allowing students to generate official-looking transcripts, performance reports, and charts that can be shared with parents or potential employers.

## Gamification & Streaks
Introduce gamified elements such as "Consistency Streaks", unlocking badges for achieving high `Academic Health` scores consistently, or meeting `Target SGPA` goals.

## University Integration (B2B)
Transition the architecture to support organizational tenants, allowing universities to deploy GradeUnderflow internally and automatically sync course structures, syllabi, and official marks directly into the platform without student manual entry.
